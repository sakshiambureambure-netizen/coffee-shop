From Farm to Cup

How We Make It Perfect

Every batch goes through our meticulous 6-step quality process.

1🌱

Farm Selection

We visit and handpick partner farms that meet our strict sustainability and quality standards.

2🫘

Harvesting

Cherries are hand-picked at peak ripeness for optimal sweetness and complexity.

3💧

Processing

Washed, natural, or honey-processed to develop unique flavor profiles.

4🔥

Roasting

Small-batch roasted to your order's specification — light, medium, or dark.

5🔬

Quality Check

Every batch is cupped and graded by our certified Q-Graders before shipping.

6📦

Fresh Delivery

Vacuum-sealed and shipped within 24 hours of roasting for maximum freshness.
